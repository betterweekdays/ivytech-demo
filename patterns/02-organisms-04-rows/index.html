Continuous Improvement Black Belt

Location: Spartanburg, SC, United States

Job # 5024608

Date Posted: 03-11-2015

CIRCOR Instrumentation is headquartered in Spartanburg, SC and specializes in fluid process control solutions with orifice sizes typically up to 2 inches. Our main product lines include HOKE valves (ball, check, needle, metering, and quick couplers), GYROLOK compression tube fittings, and GO precision pressure regulators made in a variety of materials. In addition we are responsible for CIRCOR's process sampling business, Texas Sampling. Our markets include petrochemical, refining, chemical, power, oil and gas, semiconductor, and light industrial. We have a reputation for highly engineered components, manufactured to exacting standards and quality. CIRCOR Instrumentation (CI) has over 350 employees and a global sales and distribution network.

Position Overview: The Black Belt will be accountable for planning, facilitating, and executing key projects that are focused on achieving financial results and customer satisfaction objectives. The incumbent will work closely with the leadership team to identify and drive Lean and Six Sigma initiatives to successful completion, facilitating change and providing ongoing, proactive leadership.

Essential Responsibilities:

* Leads and participates in cross-functional teams to drive breakthrough/best in class performance improvement on projects that will deliver significant financial and/or quality impact to the business.
* Works closely with functional leaders to identify opportunities for improvement and leverage the Lean Six Sigma methodology to drive improvement.
* Prepares and participates in project progress reviews with project champions and other appropriate leadership levels.
* Seeks out and secures team members and their participation on projects.
* Facilitates successful Kaizen and 5S team events.
* Identifies barriers to project success and leads efforts to eliminate the barriers.
* Prepares detailed plans to track project performance and assure timely completion and achievement of quality and saving s goals for individual projects.
* Documents improvement process learning and champions best practices with other team members.
* Has daily interaction with manufacturing employees, maintenance employees and leadership team to ensure ongoing understanding of plant focus and barriers to performance.
* Trains and mentors Green Belt project leaders in executing Lean Six Sigma projects.
* Mentors and coaches other associates in tools, techniques and Lean Six Sigma methodology.
* Performs other duties as assigned or needed.

Knowledge, Skills and Abilities Required:

* Excellent knowledge of Lean Six Sigma DMAIC methodology and analytical skills.
* Strong influencing skills and credibility to interact at all levels of the organization.
* Demonstrated success leading cross-functional teams on large projects.
* Demonstrated success working in a matrix environment and driving change through influence.
* Must possess excellent communication skills:written, verbal, and presentation.
* Project management experience including directing people, managing budgets, and creating effective time lines.
* Demonstrated strengths in project management processes and methodologies including phase gate review, planning and review methods.
* Proven track record leading Lean Six Sigma projects and kaizen events using industry standard tools and methods to deliver results.This includes providing hands on leadership of key projects and teams.
* Demonstrates a results-based leadership style with strong impact and influencing skills.
* Ability to manage and lead teams, facilitate teams and multifunctional activities.
* Demonstrated hands on ability to drive and lead projects and culture on the shop floor and at the management level.
* Ability to clearly lead change successfully and is process disciplined.
* Dynamic approach to presentation/training at all levels of an organization with the ability to translate concepts into practical application.
* Demonstrated analytical, problem solving, and change management skills.
* Must be eligible to work in the United States without employer sponsorship.

Education, Certifications, and Prior Experience Required:

* Bachelors degree in Engineering from a four-year college or university.
* Certified Six Sigma Black Belt.
* Minimum 5 years of experience in a manufacturing setting.
* 3 years of professional level experience in leading and executing continuous improvement projects.
