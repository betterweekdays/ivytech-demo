Assessment Tips

Tips

  1. The assessment is best on a desktop computer.
  2. Set aside 40 minutes to complete.
  3. If you need to scroll within the assessment, move your cursor into the frame.
  4. Progress is auto-saved at the end of each section.
  5. Starting and stopping is OK.
